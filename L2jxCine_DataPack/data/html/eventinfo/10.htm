Capture the flag
Event Information
Each team has a flag they got to protect
If a team takes enemy flag and bring it back they get scored
Team with most flags taken wins
If both teams has same score then a random team wins
Reward for each flag taken: 4 medals
Event reward: 10 medals
Event duration: 5 minutes
Levels allowed: 70-80
Minimum players: 8
Self buff event
Ressurection time: 7 sec
Rules
Be Warned!
Dualboxing is not allowed during event
Feeding enemy team is not allowed, this means buffing/going in naked etc.
In general if a char registers just to ruin event he gets risked getting punished.
