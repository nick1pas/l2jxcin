Bomb Fight
Event Information
All players get a new skill Bomb
This is the only skill you can deal dmg with
3 seconds after you put a bomb, all surrounding enemies dies and you get scored
Team with most kills wins
If both teams got same score then a random team wins
Reward for every 2 kills: 1 Medal
Event reward: 1 apigas
Event duration: 5 minutes
Levels allowed: 40-80
Minimum players: 8
Self buff event
Ressurection time: 7 Seconds
Rules
Be Warned!
Dualboxing is not allowed during event
Feeding enemy team is not allowed, this means buffing/going in naked etc.
In general if a char registers just to ruin event he gets risked getting punished.
