Battlefield
Event Information
All players get skill "Capture"
The goal is to capture as much bases as possible
For each base a team got they will get 1 point every second
Team with most score wins
If both teams got same score then a random team wins
Reward for every base capture: 2 medals
Event reward: 10 medals
Event duration: 5 minutes
Levels allowed: 70-80
Minimum players: 8
Full buff event
Ressurection time: 7 Seconds
Rules
Be Warned!
Dualboxing is not allowed during event
Feeding enemy team is not allowed, this means buffing/going in naked etc.
In general if a char registers just to ruin event he gets risked getting punished.
