Double Domination
Event Information
Team vs team event
The goal is to have more players near both Zones than the enemy team
If a team got more players in both zones for 10 seconds, they get a point
After a team gets scored, both teams gets teleported to intial locations
the team with most domination points wins
If both team got same score, then a random team wins
Event reward: 2 apigas
Levels allowed: 70-80
Event duration: 4 minutes
Minimum players: 8
Full buff event
Ressurection time: 7 sec
Rules
Be Warned!
Dualboxing is not allowed during event
Feeding enemy team is not allowed, this means buffing/going in naked etc.
In general if a char registers just to ruin event he gets risked getting punished.
