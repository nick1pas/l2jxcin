Lucky Chests
Event Information
The goal is to have most chests succesfully opened
There is a chance that a chest will explode and get you killed
The event ends when alll chests are opened or time runs out
The player with most chests opened wins
If there's more than 1 player with max score, then a random player wins
Reward for every Chest opened: 1 Medal
Event reward: 20 medals
Levels allowed: 40-80
Event duration: 3 minutes
Minimum players: 8
Self buff event
Ressurection time: 7 sec
Rules
Be Warned!
Dualboxing is not allowed during event
Feeding enemy team is not allowed, this means buffing/going in naked etc.
In general if a char registers just to ruin event he gets risked getting punished.
