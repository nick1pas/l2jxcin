Simon says
Event Information
The goal is to say exact text as the NPC says as fast as possible
If player didnt say the text within 10 seconds he's out
If player said the word wrong he is out
If player said the word last he is out
Red nick means you're out
Green nick means you said the word correct
All players are in sitting position during the event
The winner is the last person to stay with green nick
Event reward: 25 medals
Levels allowed: 40-80
Event duration: Until there's 1 winner
Minimum players: 8
Self buff event
Ressurection time: None
Rules
Be Warned!
Dualboxing is not allowed during event
Feeding enemy team is not allowed, this means buffing/going in naked etc.
In general if a char registers just to ruin event he gets risked getting punished.
