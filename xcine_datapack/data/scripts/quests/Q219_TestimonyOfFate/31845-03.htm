Bloody Pixy:
I will give you the Red Fairy Dust if you bring 10 skulls each from the Grandis, Karul Bugbear, Breka Orc Overlord, and the Leto Lizardman Overlord! Didn't you hear me swear on the jewel? Pixies always keep their promises! Would you like me to tell you once more where you can find them?!
Grandis can be found in Death Pass.
Karul Bugbears can be found in Hunters Valley.
Breka Orc Overlords are located in Breka's Stronghold.
And, Leto Lizardman Overlords are usually found on the Plains of the Lizardmen.
